Audio Projects

Below are some of my favorite audio projects that I have worked on. These projects showcase my skills in sound design, podcasting, and audio editing.

Morning Park Medley - Sound Design Project

This goal of this project was to manipulate field recordings to create a melodic souundscape. I conducted field recordings at daybreak in a local park and used Reaper to edit and layer the sounds to compose a medley. I used various effects and techniques to enhance the sounds and create a cohesive piece.

College 101 Podcast

This podcast was my Barrett Honors Thesis and Senior Capstone project. The goal of the podcast was to show that the "stereotypical" college experience is not the only college experience. In each episode, I interviewed a different college student about their unique college experience. I created and produced this podcast completely on my own, from the initial concept to the final product, using Adobe Audition for editing.

Video Projects

Photo Projects

Below are some of my favorite photography projects that I have worked on. These projects showcase my skills in photo editing, composition, and creative photography using Adobe Photoshop.

Tree Collage

For this project, I took multiple photos of plants across ASU's campus and compiled them into a tree-shaped collage using Adobe Photoshop.

Cactus Sunset

I created this photo by layering three different photos: a sun setting behind a mountain, a cactus landscape, and a starry night sky. I used Adobe Photoshop to blend the images together, manipulating the opacity of each layer, to create the effect of a sun setting in front of a row of cacti while the night sky begins to descend.

Image Projects

Below are some of my favorite graphic design projects that I have worked on. These projects showcase my skills in logo design, branding, and product design.

U of A Logo Parody

As an ASU student, I thought it would be fun to spoof our rival school's logo into an ASU logo. I wanted to keep the base recognizable as the U of A logo but change the elements to make it ASU branded. I imported the raster image of the logo into Adobe Illustrator and converted it into a vector image. I used the pen tool to trace over the original logo and rearrange the letters. I changed the colors to match ASU's branding and added the a gold pitchfork in the middle of the A.

DoubleTree New Logo

This was a logo redesign project for DoubleTree by Hilton using Canva. The goal was to modernize the logo while keeping the core elements that make it recognizable. I wanted to keep the tree as the main element of the logo but make it more stylized and contemporary. I used a sans-serif font for the text to give it a cleaner look. I also changed the color scheme to a more muted palette to give it a more sophisticated feel.

Energy Drink Design

This project was a class assignment to create a new product and design a label for it using Adobe Illustrator. I created a sports smoothie drink brand called "NRG" and designed a label for two different flavors.
